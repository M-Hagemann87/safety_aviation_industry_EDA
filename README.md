# Safety in Aviation Industry — EDA

Exploratory Data Analysis quantifying whether U.S. commercial aviation has 
become safer over the past two decades, using accident and traffic data.

## Research Questions
1. Is commercial aviation in the U.S. safer today than it was two decades ago?
2. What does the accident rate trend suggest about future safety?

## Overview
- **Type:** Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)
- **Scope:** U.S. commercial aviation, 2003–2022
- **Data Sources:**
  - Airplane Crashes & Fatalities (Kaggle)
  - U.S. Airline Traffic Data (Kaggle)

## Key Findings
- Accident rates per flight have declined significantly over the study period
- Fatality trends confirm aviation is measurably safer in 2022 vs 2003
- Traffic volume growth outpaces accident growth, reinforcing the safety trend

## Stack
Python · pandas · Matplotlib · Kaggle Datasets
